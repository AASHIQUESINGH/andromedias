Pool Buddy™
Intelligent Pool Maintenance Robot

Pool Buddy is a patented robotic system (granted in 2024) designed to automate swimming pool
maintenance. Developed as part of Andromeida’s journey in underwater robotics, Pool Buddy
reflects our expertise in creating reliable, intelligent robots for real-world water environments.
While the cleaning stack remains a work-in-progress, the true innovation lies in its automated
water chemistry optimization system, making it a first-of-its-kind solution in India.

Smarter Chemistry, Healthier Pools

Unlike conventional pool vacuums that only manage physical debris, Pool Buddy focuses on the
science of water balance. Uses a dedicated sensor stack to monitor six critical parameters: pH, TDS,
Cyanuric Acid, Temperature, Alkalinity, and Carbonate Scales. Applies the Langelier Saturation Index
(LSI) to interpret these readings and balance water chemistry on a numeric scale of –5 to +5, where 0
represents ideal pool health. This ensures optimized chemical dosing, safer water for swimmers, and
reduced operating costs for pool owners.

Field Validation

Through three working prototypes and multiple pilot deployments in residential societies across Gurgaon and Bangalore, Pool Buddy has validated its concept. Pilot stakeholders emphasized the importance of automated chemistry control in reducing manual intervention and improving pool health.

What’s Next:

Pool Buddy is currently earmarked for further R&D with two key focus areas:

  • Advancing the cleaning stack to complement water chemistry management with full robotic cleaning.
  • Integration of autonomous decision-making and dosing systems for end-to-end pool maintenance.

From Pools to Oceans

Pool Buddy is more than just a standalone product; it represents Andromeida’s roots in underwater
robotics R&D. The same spirit of innovation that built Pool Buddy has scaled into UUR (Unmanned
Underwater Robot), our flagship platform designed for broader oceanic research and industrial use
cases.

For investors and pool stakeholders, Pool Buddy stands as a proof point of Andromeida’s capacity to
develop disruptive water-domain robotics—from human pools to the vast oceans.
