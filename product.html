Meet UUR™

Our flagship product, UUR™ (Unmanned Underwater Robot), is a state-of-the-art remotely operated vehicle (RoV) built to revolutionize marine research . UUR™ is engineered as a versatile, modular platform that gives scientists and explorers an unprecedented level of customization and control underwater. Key features of UUR include:

UUR™
Engineered for Precision, Built for Possibility

From modular payloads to AI-powered imaging,
UUR adapts to your mission with unmatched control, reliability, and customization.

Modular &
Customizable Design

UUR features a universal payload mount with standardized power and communication ports, allowing seamless plug-and-play integration of sensors, cameras, and robotic tools. It’s built for researchers who want flexibility and customization at every level.

Advanced
Toolset & Real-Time SDK

With a swappable tool palette and an open SDK, UUR enables live experiment monitoring, data retrieval, and real-time adjustments. Ideal for iterative underwater research and mission-specific configurations.

Precision,
Stability & Imaging

Equipped with AI-enhanced imaging (AquaSee), advanced thrusters, and stabilization systems, UUR delivers unmatched accuracy even in strong currents or murky conditions — maximizing dive time and data quality.

Built to
Last, Built for You

UUR undergoes in-house pressure and thrust testing for durability and efficiency. Designed to meet APQP standards, it’s rugged and research-ready — and can be tailored to your exact mission, from propulsion to power systems.

UUR for Academia and Industry
Two Custom Versions

To serve the distinct needs of our users, Andromeida offers UUR in two versions: an Academic-Grade
UUR and the UUR-Quest edition. Both share the same core technology but are tailored to different operating environments and objectives.

UUR – Academic Edition

This version is optimized for universities, labs, and training purposes. It is cost-effective and highly modular, making advanced underwater robotics accessible to educational institutes and research groups on a budget. Key benefits of the Academic UUR include.

Hands-on Learning

A perfect platform for students and researchers to gain practical experience in marine robotics. The Academic UUR comes with an open architecture – both hardware and software – so users can tinker, modify, and learn by doing. It supports a variety of sensors and simple payload integration, encouraging experimentation and innovation in-house.

Lab-Friendly Design

The Academic UUR is slightly smaller and lighter, suitable for test tanks, pools, and coastal waters. Its depth rating and power are tuned for moderate environments (e.g., coastal research up to a certain depth), prioritizing ease of deployment and safety. Quick setup and intuitive controls mean researchers can focus on their experiments, not on operating the robot.

Plug-and-Play Customization

Comes with a rich library of sample payload interfaces and template code. Whether you want to attach a water quality sensor for a class project or a prototype sonar for a thesis, the Academic UUR provides plug-and-play support. Our documentation and support community further help academic users customize the robot to their research needs.

Robot-as-a-Service Option

For educational institutions, we also offer flexible leasing or Robot as-a-Service models (building on our experience with Pool Buddy’s service model ) so that even smaller colleges can time-share the robot for specific projects without a large upfront purchase.

UUR – Quest Edition

UUR-Quest is our premium, expedition-grade model, built for marine industry professionals, serious explorers, and advanced research missions. It retains UUR’s modularity and precision, but augments it for tougher challenges.

Enhanced Performance

UUR-Quest is equipped with more powerful thrusters and advanced navigation systems, allowing it to operate in harsher open-water conditions and stronger currents. It has a higher depth rating, making it suitable for deep-sea exploration and offshore industry use.

Heavy Payload Capacity

With a reinforced frame and upgraded buoyancy control, the Quest edition can carry heavier and more complex payloads. This means it can support sophisticated tools – multi-beam sonars, robotic arms for sample collection, high-end cameras, or even multiple sensors at once – catering to professional survey and inspection tasks.

Rugged & Reliable

Each UUR-Quest undergoes rigorous testing in our pressure chamber well beyond standard operating depths to ensure a high safety margin. Its electronics are hardened for long-duration missions. The Quest is also built with redundancy in critical systems (power, communications) to meet the reliability demands of commercial operations like aquaculture inspection, underwater construction, or environmental monitoring.

Real-Time Data & Control

Designed with industry in mind, UUR-Quest integrates seamlessly with vessel systems and on-shore control centers. Over its tether (or optional fiber-optic link), it streams HD video and sensor data in real time to operators. This live feed capability, combined with precision control, makes UUR-Quest ideal for situations where decisions must be made on the spot – for example, inspecting an underwater pipeline and immediately responding to what’s found.
