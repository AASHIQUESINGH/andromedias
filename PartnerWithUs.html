Partnerships & Collaborations
Driving Impact Together

Andromeida’s journey is fueled by strong collaborations and the trust of key partners
and investors. We work closely with government, academia, and industry
stakeholders to amplify our impact.

Ministry of Electronics & IT (MeitY)

We have an MoU with the Foundation for Innovation and Technology Transfer (FITT) at IIT Delhi and the Ministry of Electronics & IT (MeitY), formalizing a collaboration to advance underwater robotic technology . This partnership not only validates our R&D capabilities but also helps bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world applications, with support from national innovation programs.

NIOT (Ministry of Earth Sciences)

Our connection with NIOT (Ministry of Earth Sciences), as mentioned earlier, is a cornerstone of our R&D collaboration. Winning the NIOT tender for AUV control systems has positioned Andromeida as a go-to private player for specialized underwater projects. Through this, we contribute to national projects like the Deep Ocean Mission by providing our expertise in robotics and AI.

Global Affiliations

We actively engage with international forums and organizations in marine technology. Our team is building relationships akin to those of other marine robotics pioneers, sharing knowledge and aligning with the growth of the global blue economy. (Inspired by global collaborations, much like Armada Marine Robotics’ affiliations with WHOI and others , we seek partnerships worldwide. We believe that solving ocean challenges is a collective effort, and we’re open to collaborating on research initiatives, pilot projects, and technology integrations that further ocean exploration.

Investors and Backers

Andromeida is fortunate to be trusted by industry leaders, government bodies, and impact investors globally . Our supporters include Impact Invest VC, the Ministry of Electronics & IT, MeitY Startup Hub (MSH), and FITT, among others . Their backing attests to our vision and execution. We ensure transparent communication and value creation for our investors, as we work towards scaling production and expanding our product line.
